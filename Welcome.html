WELCOME!

Blue Fever are delighted to welcome you all to this evening, celebrating our 10th year at The Club and thank you all for supporting both Blue Fever and community arts within Abu Dhabi.

The members of Blue Fever are very grateful to The Club, its staff and to Arun Babu (Events & Banqueting Manager) in particular, for their support and assistance in bringing this event to you.

We are joined this evening by our friends; the accomplished, professional musicians of Retromantics. Together, we promise you a fun-filled, entertaining evening.

THANK YOU!

Our supremely talented Musical Director Helen Kerr, who is leading us for the second year and ensures.
